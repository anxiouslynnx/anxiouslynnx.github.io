About Elysian Realm

Elysian Realm is a group of passionate League of Legends players, each contributing their skills and dedication across various levels of play. Whether it’s casual fun, competitive grinding, or pushing through tournaments and events, Elysian Realm caters to different needs and goals within the game. We offer four distinct services:

1. Play for Fun (Free): Connect with fellow gamers in our Discord community to enjoy casual games together. Perfect for those looking to relax and have fun.

2. Grinding for Rank: We don’t just accept any request; we evaluate a player’s current and past MMR to ensure that smurfing won’t ruin the experience for others. This option is for serious players focused on climbing the ranks.

3.Tournaments (Free): Participate in free tournaments. Just like the "Play for Fun" option, you’ll be part of a community of players working together to dominate the competition.

4. Event Farming: Busy with life but still want those precious event rewards? We’ll help grind them for you, ensuring you don’t miss out on limited-time rewards.
